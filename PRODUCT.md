# Product

## Register

product

## Users

University students and recent graduates learning how to move from education into data careers through realistic, guided practice.

## Product Purpose

Plato Data Simulator turns the graduate-to-work transition into a playable career story. Players build professional materials, search and apply for jobs, experience realistic hiring outcomes, complete interviews and assessments, and begin guided workplace projects.

## Brand Personality

Realistic, encouraging, and absorbing. The experience should feel like a well-produced career game while preserving the familiarity and credibility of real communication, hiring, and workplace tools.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic dashboard templates, decorative SaaS landing pages, childish gamification, predetermined conversations that ignore context, unrealistic instant success, and interfaces that explain themselves instead of behaving like the real tools they represent.

## Design Principles

- Make every stage feel consequential and connected to the player's developing story.
- Teach through realistic action, feedback, waiting, success, and failure.
- Use familiar product patterns so the simulation feels credible immediately.
- Keep emotional moments focused and cinematic; keep work surfaces practical.
- Let the player's CV and professional profile grow from demonstrated experience.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA, maintain keyboard access and readable contrast, support reduced motion, provide text alternatives for voice content, and avoid relying on color alone for status.
